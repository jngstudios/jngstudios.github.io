# Lumen RSS/News Reader: Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

Lumen is an RSS/Atom feed reader. Your privacy is straightforward to explain: we do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Data stored on your device

All data — your feed subscriptions, articles, read status, starred items, and preferences — is stored locally on your device using your device's storage. It is never sent to us. If your device has backup configured (e.g. to facilitate moving to new devices) your device provider could be keeping a copy of this data.

## Network requests

When you add or refresh a feed, the app fetches the RSS or Atom feed directly from the URL you provided. No other network requests are made. The app does not contact any server operated by the developer.

## No analytics or tracking

The app contains no analytics, no crash-reporting services, no advertising SDKs, and no tracking of any kind.

## No accounts

The app does not require or support user accounts. No login, no email address, nothing.

## Data deletion

You can delete all your data at any time using the "Delete all feeds" option in Settings, or by uninstalling the app.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us via the app's listing on the Google Play Store.
